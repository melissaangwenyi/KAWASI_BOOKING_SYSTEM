About Kawasi Hostels

Welcome to Kawasi hostels, a vibrant community nestled just a short stroll away from the University of Nairobi, Kenyatta University, Kabarack University and Strathmore university. Our vibrant community will be home to an extensive student community from esteemed universities and colleges like the University of Nairobi, Aga Khan University, Edulink International College, Graffins College, Bridge College, Baristapro Kenya, Oshwal College, ADMI, Nairobi Bar School, Kenya Methodist University, Nairobi Institute of Technology (N.I.T), and many more. With such diversity, we provide a much-needed option for student accommodation in the Riverside area.
