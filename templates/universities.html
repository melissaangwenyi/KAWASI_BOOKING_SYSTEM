List of Kenyan Universities

  • University of Nairobi:

    The University of Nairobi is a collegiate research university based in Nairobi. It is the largest and oldest university in Kenya.

  • Kenyatta University:

    Kenyatta University is a public university located in Nairobi. It is one of the largest universities in Kenya.

  • Moi University:

    Moi University is a public university located in Eldoret. It was established in 1984.

  • Egerton University:

    Egerton University is a public university located in Njoro, Nakuru County. It was established in 1939.

  • Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT):

    JKUAT is a public university located in Juja, near Nairobi. It specializes in technology and agriculture-related courses.

  • KCA University:

    KCA University is a private university located in Nairobi. It offers various courses in business, technology, and related fields.

  • Strathmore University:

    Strathmore University is a private university located in Nairobi. It is known for its business, accountancy, and finance programs.

  • United States International University Africa (USIU-Africa):

    USIU-Africa is a private university located in Nairobi. It offers American-style education with global perspectives.

  • Daystar University:

    Daystar University is a private university with campuses in Nairobi and Athi River. It is a Christian university offering various programs.

  • African Nazarene University (ANU):

    ANU is a private Christian university located in Ongata Rongai, Nairobi. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Kenya Methodist University (KEMU):

    KEMU is a private university located in Meru, with campuses in Nairobi and Mombasa. It offers programs in various fields.

  • Maseno University:

    Maseno University is a public university located in Maseno, Kisumu County. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Mount Kenya University (MKU):

    MKU is a private university with campuses across Kenya. It offers programs in various disciplines.

  • Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST):

    JOOUST is a public university located in Bondo, Siaya County. It offers programs in science and technology.

  • Technical University of Kenya (TU-K):

    TU-K is a public technical university located in Nairobi. It offers programs in engineering, applied sciences, and technology.

  • Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT):

    DeKUT is a public university located in Nyeri. It specializes in science, engineering, and technology-related courses.

  • Kisii University:

    Kisii University is a public university located in Kisii. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Cooperative University of Kenya:

    The Cooperative University of Kenya is a public university located in Karen, Nairobi. It specializes in cooperative-related education.

  • Pwani University:

    Pwani University is a public university located in Kilifi. It offers programs in various disciplines.

  • South Eastern Kenya University (SEKU):

    SEKU is a public university located in Kitui. It offers programs in arts, science, and education.

  • University of Eldoret:

    The University of Eldoret is a public university located in Eldoret. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Taita Taveta University (TTU):

    TTU is a public university located in Voi. It offers programs in science, technology, and education.

  • Laikipia University:

  • Rongo University:

    Rongo University is a public university located in Rongo, Migori County. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

  • Murang'a University of Technology (MUT):

    MUT is a public university located in Murang'a. It specializes in technology and engineering-related courses.

  • Meru University of Science and Technology (MUST):

    MUST is a public university located in Meru. It offers programs in science, technology, and education.
